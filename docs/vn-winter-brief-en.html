01 · Why Vietnam, why now

Koreans' #1 in Southeast Asia — and winter is coming

Not budget sightseeing — resort stays. 4+ night resort bookings are up 20% YoY and family travel keeps rising. 4.5M+ Korean visitors projected this year, heading straight into the winter peak.

✈

Proximity

Multiple 4–5h direct flights from Seoul to Hanoi, HCMC, Da Nang and Phu Quoc. FX and fuel costs flipped the equation vs Guam/Saipan. New Incheon routes from Aug 25.

🏝

Resort stays

Stays, not sightseeing, drive this market. Agoda's Korean top 10 features three Vietnam spots — Nha Trang (#4), Da Nang (#6), Phu Quoc (#10). Resort staycations even in summer.

🛂

Visa-free

45 days visa-free nationwide for Koreans, guaranteed through 2028 — the only short-haul resort destination where long-stay products are this easy to build.

❄

Winter = main stage

Southern dry season (Nov–Apr) meets Korea's Lunar New Year and winter school holidays. Phu Quoc 4–5★ resorts near sold out (90%) over Tet — the best-selling season, with the fiercest competition for inventory.
